Method
Complexity → Impact

The work I do
moves products from complexity to clarity.

01 · Complexity

The state I get hired into.

14 data sources. 200 retail locations. Five years of feature debt. The interfaces have stopped reflecting the system.

02 · Structure

Research and architecture.

Interviews, journey maps, IA, state logic. The frame the design will live inside, before any pixels.

03 · Clarity

The interface itself.

One canvas instead of three tools. Progressive disclosure instead of tab sprawl. State-aware patterns, documented.

04 · Impact

The number that came back.

23 → 8 minutes per segment. −30% checkout time. −45% payroll errors. Measured, not asserted.
